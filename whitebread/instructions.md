Portions:
  - 650 flour
  - 350 water
  - 200 starter
  - 15 salt

Instructions: 
- Mix flour water salt and starter at the end
- Fold at 1 hour
- Prove for 5 hours
- Put dutch oven in oven
- Start heating oven for 30 after the proving
- Leave cooking for 40 minutes
- Remove lid for 7 minutes to cris the top
